## How the Game Works

- The story is divided into **six acts**
- Each act is played as either:
  - **Past Self** (making choices)
  - **Future Self** (handling consequences)
- Your choices permanently alter:
  - The world
  - Characters
  - The ending

You cannot see all outcomes in one playthrough.
